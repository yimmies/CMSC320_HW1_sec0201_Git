Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: [It is useful for keeping track of updates over time and serves as a backup incase there are issues saving or retrieving the information. It is also helpful with isolating individual work when multiple users are working on the repository.]

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: [Data science could be applied to the medical field. Using information from a hospital database, we could use patient information to identify common markers in certain conditions or diseases and attempt to predict them.]
